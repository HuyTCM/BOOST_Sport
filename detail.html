BUILT FOR SHARP CUTS

The Kyrie 3 Men's Basketball Shoe combines excellent traction, flexible support and responsive cushioning to enable razor-sharp cuts and fast, fluid play.

EXCELLENT TRACTION

The rounded sole and extra traction along the edges of the shoe let you play at any angle. Two pods beneath the forefoot grip the ground hard when you change direction fast.

FLEXIBLE SUPPORT

Across the top of the shoe, a strong, stretchy band flexes with your foot for support during quick cuts and sprints. Flywire technology—ultralight, super strong cables that integrate with the laces—adds even more support.

RESPONSIVE CUSHIONING

In the heel, low-profile Nike Zoom Air cushioning is incredibly responsive for game-long comfort.
